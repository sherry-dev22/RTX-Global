Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Privacy Policy

RXT Global Solutions - Operating Division of RXT Global BPO & Marketing Inc.

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

RXT Global Solutions ("the Company," "we," "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services or visit our website.

This policy applies to all clients, prospects, and website users of RXT Global Solutions, operating as a division of RXT Global BPO & Marketing Inc., a registered Canadian corporation.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services:

Personal Information

  • Name: First name, last name, and business title
  • Contact Information: Email address, phone number, business address
  • Business Information: Company name, industry, business size, role
  • Communication Preferences: Preferred contact methods and times

Payment Information

  • Billing Details: Invoicing address, payment method preferences
  • Transaction Data: Payment amounts, dates, service packages
  • Important: We do not store credit card or sensitive payment details directly

Service-Related Information

  • Service Requirements: Specific needs, goals, and expectations
  • Performance Data: Service usage statistics and outcomes
  • Feedback: Survey responses, testimonials, and improvement suggestions

Technical Information

  • Website Usage: IP address, browser type, device information
  • Cookies: Session data and preferences
  • Analytics: Pages visited, time spent, referral sources

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

Service Delivery

  • To provide agreed-upon B2B services
  • To customize services to your specific business needs
  • To coordinate with our affiliated service providers
  • To monitor and improve service quality

Communication

  • To respond to inquiries and requests
  • To send service updates and notifications
  • To provide customer support
  • To share relevant business insights and industry information

Billing and Administration

  • To process payments and issue invoices
  • To maintain accurate financial records
  • To comply with accounting and tax requirements
  • To manage service subscriptions and renewals

Legal and Security

  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and property
  • To ensure website and service security
  • To prevent fraud and unauthorized activities

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

Payment Processors

We share payment-related information with trusted third-party payment processors:

  • Stripe: For credit card and online payment processing
  • Moneris: For Canadian payment processing services
  • Important: These processors handle payment information according to PCI DSS standards

Service Providers

  • Affiliated brands and service providers within our corporate structure
  • Third-party vendors necessary for service delivery
  • Professional advisors (legal, accounting, consulting)

Legal Requirements

  • When required by law, regulation, or court order
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • To investigate suspected wrongdoing
  • In connection with business transfers or mergers

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted during transmission and storage
  • Access Controls: Limited access to authorized personnel only
  • Secure Systems: Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Employee Training: Staff trained on privacy and security protocols
  • Compliance: Adherence to Canadian privacy laws (PIPEDA)

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill our contractual obligations
  • Comply with legal requirements
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Maintain business records

Retention periods vary based on the type of information and legal requirements. When data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

7. Your Rights

Under Canadian privacy law, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent where appropriate
  • Complaint: File a complaint with privacy authorities

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information provided below.

8. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Provide personalized content where appropriate

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

9. International Data Transfers

As a Canadian company, we primarily store and process data within Canada. However, some third-party service providers may process data outside Canada. We ensure such providers maintain adequate privacy protections.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for business use only. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18 years of age. If we become aware of such collection, we will promptly delete the information.

11. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via email or website notices. Your continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
